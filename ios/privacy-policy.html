Privacy Policy for HIIT Life

Effective Date: May 7, 2026
Last Updated: July 21, 2026

Introduction

HIIT Life ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our fitness application.

The short version: HIIT Life is a 100% offline fitness app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information with our servers or third parties. All your data stays on your device.

Age Restriction

This app is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect or store data from anyone under the age of 16. If you are under 16, please do not use this app.

While the app does not collect data from anyone, high-intensity interval training (HIIT) is a physically demanding activity that may not be appropriate for younger users. Parents and guardians should supervise minors and consult with a healthcare professional before allowing anyone under 18 to engage in high-intensity exercise.

Please review our Terms of Service for important medical disclaimers regarding high-intensity exercise.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we do not do:

  • ❌ We do NOT collect personal identifying information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or login
  • ❌ We do NOT track your location
  • ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • ❌ We do NOT share data with third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT use advertising networks
  • ❌ We do NOT transmit your data to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • ❌ We do NOT collect payment information (the app is free)
  • ❌ We do NOT access your photos, contacts, or other personal files

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

HIIT Life stores ALL data locally on your device ONLY. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on any external server. The developer has no access to any user data whatsoever.

The following types of data are stored exclusively on your device to provide app functionality:

1. Workout Data

  • Custom workout names and descriptions you create
  • Exercise lists
  • Workout media URLs (images/videos) you choose to add
  • Workout creation timestamps

2. Timer Configurations

  • Timer names and settings you create
  • Interval durations (warmup, work, rest, cooldown)
  • Round and set counts
  • Timer creation timestamps

3. Supplement Tracking Data

  • Supplement names, types, and servings you track
  • Consumption logs with timestamps
  • Low stock thresholds you set
  • Scheduled reminder times you configure

4. App Preferences

  • Notification settings
  • Sound and voice announcement preferences
Important: All this data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure local storage (on-device SQLite storage and the iOS Keychain). This data never leaves your device unless you manually share it (e.g., screenshots, backups). The developer has no access to any of your workout data, supplement tracking, or user preferences.

Data Loss Warning

Because ALL data is stored locally on your device, the developer CANNOT recover lost data under any circumstances.

You may permanently lose your data if:

  • You uninstall the app from your device
  • Your device storage is cleared or the app's data is deleted
  • Your device is damaged, lost, stolen, or erased
  • Your device's operating system is reinstalled or updated in a way that clears app data
  • You switch to a new device without manually transferring data (or without restoring from an iCloud/device backup that includes app data)

You are solely responsible for backing up your data if you wish to preserve it. The developer recommends periodically recording important workout configurations and supplement schedules if you want to retain them long-term.

How Your Data is Used

Your locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your custom workouts, timers, and supplement schedules
  • Execute HIIT timer sessions
  • Provide scheduled notifications for supplement reminders
  • Save your preferences and settings

Since all data is local, we never see, access, or process your information.

Internet Usage & Third-Party Content

HIIT Life uses internet access for one optional feature only:

Loading Remote Workout Media (Optional)

When you choose to add YouTube video URLs or remote image URLs to your workouts, the app:

  • Fetches publicly available YouTube thumbnail images from https://img.youtube.com
  • Displays remote images/videos from URLs you provide

What this means:

  • You control whether internet is used (by choosing whether to add remote media)
  • No personal data is transmitted when loading these images
  • The app functions 100% offline if you don't add remote media
  • YouTube/image servers only see a standard request for a public image (like visiting the URL in a browser)

All other app features work completely offline.

Permissions Explained

HIIT Life may request the following permissions on iOS:

Permission Purpose Required?
Notifications Display supplement reminders and low-stock alerts Optional

You can deny notification permissions if you don't want supplement reminders. The app will still function for timer and workout features. Haptic feedback during timer countdowns uses standard iOS system haptics and does not require a separate permission.

No Analytics, Tracking, or Data Collection

HIIT Life does NOT use any analytics tools, tracking technologies, or data collection mechanisms.

Specifically, the app does NOT:

  • ❌ Use analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ❌ Include tracking pixels or beacons
  • ❌ Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • ❌ Include advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • ❌ Collect usage statistics or telemetry data
  • ❌ Monitor your workout sessions, frequency, or patterns
  • ❌ Track your location, device information, or behavior
  • ❌ Send crash reports or diagnostic data to external servers

No usage data is collected whatsoever. We don't know who uses the app, how often it's used, which features are popular, or any other metrics. Your privacy is absolute. HIIT Life does not use the iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework because it performs no tracking of any kind.

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • Workout and timer data: Stored in on-device, app-sandboxed storage
  • Sensitive data: Uses the iOS Keychain with hardware-backed encryption when available

No Cloud Vulnerability

Since we don't use cloud servers or transmit data:

  • No risk of data breaches from server hacks
  • No risk of unauthorized access to our databases
  • No risk of third-party data leaks
Your data never leaves your device, so it cannot be intercepted, stolen, or accessed by us or third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

How Long We Keep Data

We don't keep any data because we don't collect or store anything on our servers.

How Long Your Device Keeps Data

Your device stores your workout, timer, and supplement data until:

  • You manually delete individual items using the delete buttons in the app
  • You delete the app's data via iOS Settings > General > iPhone Storage > HIIT Life > Delete App
  • You uninstall the app

Note: Deleting the app's data through iOS Settings or uninstalling the app will permanently erase all your workouts, timers, and supplement tracking history. This action cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services

HIIT Life does NOT integrate with any third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising.

YouTube Thumbnails

When you add a YouTube URL to a workout, the app fetches a thumbnail from YouTube's public CDN (img.youtube.com). This is a standard image request that does not send any of your personal data to YouTube. YouTube may log the image request with basic technical information (IP address, timestamp) per their own privacy policy, but this is not linked to your identity or HIIT Life account (since you don't have one).

Parents and guardians should:

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

Right to Know: We don't collect personal information, so there's nothing to disclose.

Right to Delete: Since we don't store your data on our servers, there's nothing for us to delete. You control deletion by clearing app data on your device.

Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We don't sell personal information (we don't collect any).

Non-Discrimination: We don't discriminate based on exercising privacy rights.

Since HIIT Life doesn't collect, store, or process personal information, these rights are automatically satisfied.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Legal Basis for Processing: Not applicable - we don't process personal data.

Data Subject Rights:

  • Right to access: We don't store your data
  • Right to rectification: You control your local data
  • Right to erasure: Clear app data on your device
  • Right to restrict processing: Not applicable
  • Right to data portability: Your data is already on your device
  • Right to object: Not applicable

Data Protection Officer: Not required - we don't process personal data.

Supervisory Authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, though we don't process personal data.
